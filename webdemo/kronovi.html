Kronovi

Cameron "Kronovi" Bills is an American Rocket League player and streamer. He has played for G2 Esports since September 2016. He has been in RLCS League Play four times, the RLCS World Championships twice, and is an RLCS S1 World Champion. In addition to being a top player, Kronovi and his team are popular personalities and fan favorites, especially in the NA region.
